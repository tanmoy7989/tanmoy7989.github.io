Created Amgen's first cloud-native, end-to-end MLOps pipeline dedicated to supervised biophysical property prediction from protein sequences. The pipeline is currently used across 8+ antibody design projects and has been extended to mentor junior data scientists in developing MLOps workflows for protein thermostability prediction from sequence.

Key capabilities include automated training, evaluation, and deployment of sequence-to-property models on AWS Batch, with experiment tracking and model versioning integrated into the antibody design workflow.
