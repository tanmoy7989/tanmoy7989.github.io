Developed cloud-native parallelized ODE solvers for pharmacokinetic modeling of bispecific molecules as part of Amgen's multispecifics design platform. The ODE integration was deployed via ML regression, achieving a 50× speedup over direct numerical integration while maintaining accuracy across candidate molecules.

This work enables rapid screening of bispecific architectures during early design and connects to ongoing experimental validation of pharmacokinetic predictions on bispecific candidates.
