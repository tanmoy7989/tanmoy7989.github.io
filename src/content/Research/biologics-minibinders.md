Designed mini-binders for a rare disease target using Rosetta-ddg and a RFDiffusion / ProteinMPNN / AlphaFold workflow. An active-learning pipeline with Gaussian process regression achieved 20× affinity improvement over three design iterations.

The pipeline integrates structure prediction, sequence design, and experimental feedback into a closed loop for iterative binder optimization — bridging generative protein design with quantitative affinity modeling.
