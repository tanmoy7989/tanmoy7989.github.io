Developed linear-programming algorithms to optimize degenerate codon libraries using protein language models for in-silico display library design. The workflow set an internal record for fastest request-to-delivery (~3 days) for a 1M+ diverse antibody library.

The approach combines combinatorial optimization with sequence representations from protein language models to maximize library diversity while respecting synthesis and experimental constraints.
